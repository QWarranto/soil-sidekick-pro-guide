## Use Cases

### 1. Continuous Crop Canopy Monitoring
**Skyline**: mmWave radar detects subtle changes in canopy reflectivity (water content, structural stress)  
**LeafEngines**: Correlates radar signatures with soil data, weather patterns, and satellite imagery to diagnose root cause

**Outcome**: Detect crop stress **days before visible symptoms** appear

---

### 2. Precision Equipment Synchronization
**Skyline**: GPS-independent timing for autonomous farm equipment in RF-challenged environments  
**LeafEngines**: Real-time field boundary and prescription map delivery to synchronized equipment

**Outcome**: Sub-meter accuracy for variable rate application **without GPS dependency**

---

### 3. Agricultural Worker Safety
**Skyline**: Wireless vital signs monitoring (heat stress, fatigue, fall detection)  
**LeafEngines**: Environmental risk correlation (heat index, air quality, chemical exposure windows)

**Outcome**: Proactive safety alerts **before incidents occur**

---

### 4. Environmental Hazard Detection
**Skyline**: RF spectrum monitoring for interference sources, weather anomalies  
**LeafEngines**: EPA water quality, satellite vegetation indices, soil contamination risk

**Outcome**: Comprehensive environmental threat dashboard with **<60 second latency**
