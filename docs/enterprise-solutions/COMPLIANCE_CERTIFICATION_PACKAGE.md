# Compliance & Certification Package
## LeafEngines Enterprise Solution Briefing Sheet

**Service Code:** LE-ENT-CCP

## Executive Summary

The Compliance & Certification Package provides enterprise customers with comprehensive audit trails, regulatory documentation, third-party certification support, and compliance monitoring infrastructure. This solution enables customers in regulated industries to leverage LeafEngines' botanical intelligence while meeting stringent regulatory and certification requirements.

---

## Customer Qualification Criteria

### Ideal Customer Profile

Customers who derive maximum value from compliance services typically exhibit:

| Criterion | Indicator | Benefit Multiplier |
|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Pharmaceutical/Nutraceutical** | GMP, FDA 21 CFR Part 11 requirements | 4.5x |
| **Government Contractor** | FedRAMP, FISMA, StateRAMP requirements | 4.0x |
| **Financial Services** | SOX, PCI-DSS audit requirements | 3.5x |
| **Healthcare Adjacent** | HIPAA BAA requirements | 3.5x |
| **International Trade** | CITES, phytosanitary certification | 3.0x |
| **Insurance/Legal** | Evidence chain, expert witness support | 2.8x |

### Trigger Conditions

The customer should consider the Compliance Package when:

1. **Audit Requirement** - External auditors require vendor compliance documentation
2. **Certification Pursuit** - Customer pursuing ISO, SOC 2, or industry certification
3. **Regulatory Filing** - Botanical data used in regulatory submissions (FDA, USDA)
4. **Legal Evidence** - Plant identification used in litigation or insurance claims
5. **Supply Chain Compliance** - Vendor risk management programs require attestations
6. **Export Controls** - CITES or phytosanitary documentation requirements

### Disqualifying Factors

This solution may NOT be appropriate when:

- No regulatory or certification requirements
- Internal use only with no audit exposure
- Budget constraints below $75K annually
- Customer unable to implement complementary internal controls
