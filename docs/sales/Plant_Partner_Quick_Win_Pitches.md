## 2. Fast Growing Trees

### Company Profile

- **Segment**: Online nursery - trees, shrubs, fruit trees
- **Scale**: 2.6M+ customers, 70,000+ 5-star reviews
- **Differentiator**: 1-year guarantee, zone-filtered recommendations

### Customer Pain Points (from public reviews)

| Pain Point | Severity | Business Impact |
|------------|----------|-----------------|
| High prices | Medium | Cart abandonment |
| Plants arrive in poor condition | High | Returns, guarantee claims |
| Planting uncertainty | High | Customer anxiety, failures |
| Post-purchase failure | High | Guarantee costs, negative reviews |

### LeafEngines Solution: "Plant Success Guarantee+"

**The core problem**: USDA Hardiness Zones are too coarse. Zone 7 covers Virginia to Oklahoma—completely different soil, weather, and growing conditions. Generic zone filtering leads to preventable failures.

**What we enable** (outcomes, not methods):

1. **Pre-Purchase Compatibility Scoring**
   - Customer sees success probability for their specific address
   - Soil, climate, and regional factors considered before purchase
   - Alternative suggestions when compatibility is low
   - *Result*: Right plant, right place—fewer failures

2. **Location-Specific Planting Guidance**
   - Exact planting windows based on customer's local frost dates
   - Soil preparation tailored to regional soil composition
   - First-year care calendar for their specific conditions
   - *Result*: Customers plant at the right time, in the right way

3. **Proactive Failure Prevention**
   - Weather alerts relevant to new plantings
   - Regional pest/disease early warnings
   - Intervention guidance before problems become fatal
   - *Result*: Guarantee claims drop significantly

4. **Expansion Recommendations**
   - "What else thrives at your address" suggestions
   - Compatibility scores for entire catalog
   - *Result*: Higher repeat purchases, larger orders

### Business Case

| Metric | Current State | Projected with LeafEngines |
|--------|---------------|---------------------------|
| Guarantee claim rate | ~5-8% estimated | 3% or less |
| Cart abandonment | High on expensive items | Reduced with confidence scoring |
| Return customer rate | ~20% | 35%+ |
| Average order value | $80-120 | Higher with compatibility-confirmed upsells |

### Investment & ROI Summary

- **Pro Tier**: $12,000/year (supports pre-purchase + post-purchase care)
- **Cost Savings**: 3% claim reduction on 2.6M customers = $3.9M in avoided replacements
- **Revenue Lift**: Confidence-based purchasing adds $2M+ conservatively
- **ROI**: 490:1

### Company Profile

- **Scale**: 100,000+ households
- **Price Point**: $400-$900 hardware + $49/month membership
- **Differentiator**: AI-powered growing ("Kelby"), camera/sensor monitoring

| Pain Point | Severity | Business Impact |
|------------|----------|-----------------|

### LeafEngines Solution: "Kelby Enhanced Intelligence"

**The opportunity**: Gardyn's sensors measure what's happening *inside* the unit. LeafEngines adds awareness of what's happening *outside*—local water quality, seasonal context, regional growing conditions.

**What we enable** (outcomes, not methods):

1. **Local Water Quality Optimization**
   - Automatic adjustments based on regional water characteristics

2. **Seasonal Engagement & Value Demonstration**
