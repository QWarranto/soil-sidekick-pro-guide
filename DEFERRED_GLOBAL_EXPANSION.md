## Lessons Learned

### What to Do Differently Next Time
1. **Revenue First**: Prove market fit domestically before international expansion
2. **Incremental Approach**: Launch one region at a time, not multiple simultaneously
3. **Budget Buffer**: Maintain 12 months runway before major new initiatives
4. **Customer Validation**: Get 5+ paid US customers before expanding

### What Was Done Right
1. **Complete Planning**: Detailed specs ready for execution when budget allows
2. **Technical Foresight**: Architecture supports global from day 1
3. **Strategic Flexibility**: Able to pivot quickly based on business reality
4. **No Sunk Costs**: Minimal investment before market validation
